Throughout the internship, I had the privilege to attend three community events organized by the First Cloud Journey (FCAJ) program. These meetups provided valuable learning opportunities that blended technical insights, soft skills training, and networking with IT professionals.

### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)
&emsp;**Event Name:** FCAJ Community Day — 09/05/2026

&emsp;**Date & Time:** 9:00AM 09/05/2026

&emsp;**Location:** Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Role:** Participant

&emsp;**Summary:** A technical gathering featuring 4 talks: hacking learning psychology and leveraging dopamine to build sustainable habits (Huynh Hoang Long), advanced Prompt Engineering practices alongside a Proptimizer demo on AWS Serverless (Nguyen Tuan Thinh), the vital role of core computing fundamentals for juniors in the generative AI era (Nguyen Duy Khang), and introducing a structured, AI-assisted software development workflow using the BMAD Method (Thao Nguyen).

### [Event 2](4.2-Event2/)
&emsp;**Event Name:** FCAJ Community Meetup — 30/05/2026

&emsp;**Date & Time:** 9:00AM 30/05/2026

&emsp;**Location:** Ho Chi Minh City

&emsp;**Role:** Participant

&emsp;**Summary:** A collaborative meetup featuring 7 speakers sharing key insights: gamified cloud training with AWS Cloud Quest and Floci (Huynh Thai Linh), applying the AWS Builder mindset to tackle procrastination (Pham Khac Uy), strategies for building self-confidence in IT careers (Nguyen Thi Quynh Nhu), cloud architecture design for real-time multiplayer gaming (Pham Quang Thai), building a modern astrology platform (Tran Huu Nghia), focusing on the human factor under the DevOps iceberg (Tran Minh Quan), and key takeaways from developing the SynthHunter AI voice authentication hackathon project (Team The Ballers).
