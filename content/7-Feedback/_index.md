> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud Journey program. This will help the FCJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment at FCJ is very open and flexible. Due to the nature of working extensively with Cloud systems, being able to arrange lab time flexibly helped me optimize my performance. However, it would be wonderful if there were short exchange sessions between interns to share debugging experiences during practice.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentor was very dedicated, especially in complex architectural parts like setting up VPC or configuring IAM permissions. Instead of just giving the answer, the mentor usually suggested ways to self-debug errors when writing functions. The admin team was also very enthusiastic in supporting accounts and addressing arising issues.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The internship content was extremely close to my Software Engineering major. Being able to personally deploy infrastructure from scratch, connect Backend Node.js with the database, and deploy it to the AWS environment strongly reinforced my knowledge of networks, databases, and software design learned at university.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
In addition to soft skills like time management and progress reporting (worklog), I was truly impressed by the volume of specialized knowledge received. I learned how to deploy Serverless services, optimize NoSQL databases, and better understand real-world CI/CD processes instead of just coding in a local environment.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The working culture is highly respectful and constructive. When the system encounters errors or during difficult labs, everyone is willing to share screens to find the root cause together. I felt like I was contributing as a real member rather than just an apprentice.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The company provides full resources (AWS accounts/credits) so that interns can practice comfortably without worrying about incurring costs. The flexible time also made it easy for me to balance the internship with other courses at university.


### Additional Questions
- **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
  What satisfied me the most was being empowered to design the architecture and execute projects using modern technology (Node.js, Serverless architecture). Transitioning from theory to a running, real-world system that can be monitored via a dashboard gave me a great sense of accomplishment.
- **What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**  
  The volume of videos and labs can sometimes be overwhelming if condensed into a short period. The company could consider spacing out the timeline or providing worklog templates that break down tasks week by week (like a 12-week roadmap) so that future interns do not feel overwhelmed and have time to dive deeper into each service.
- **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**  
  Definitely yes. Especially for students who want to dive deep into Cloud Computing and Backend, this is a very good practical environment to build a strong portfolio with highly applicable projects.


### Suggestions & Expectations
- **Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**  
  There should be additional cross-group "Showcase" or "Code Review" sessions at the end of each week or phase. Seeing how others design UI/UX or write backend logic would help expand problem-solving thinking.
- **Would you like to continue this program in the future?**  
  I am very willing to continue if given the opportunity to participate in more specialized projects or try out for official positions after graduation.
- **Any other comments (free sharing):**  
  Thank you, FCJ team, for creating an internship roadmap that closely matches today's industry demands. These past experiences are very valuable assets for my future career path.
